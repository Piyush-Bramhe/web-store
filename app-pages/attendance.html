About this app

Attendance App is designed to simplify daily attendance tracking with a fast, reliable, and distraction-free experience. It helps users maintain accurate records while reducing manual effort, making it suitable for classrooms, coaching institutes, and personal use.

Key features

  • Quick one-tap attendance marking
  • Automatic date and session tracking
  • Clear present and absent separation
  • Works completely offline
  • Lightweight and fast performance
  • Clean and easy-to-use interface
